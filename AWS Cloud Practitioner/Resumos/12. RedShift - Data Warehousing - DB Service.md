OLTP vs OLAP
	- **OLTP - Online Transactional Processing**
		- Regular queries on teh Databases
		- **Ex:** details from order #4341124
	- **OLAP - Online Analytical Processing**
		- Pull in **large** numbers of Records
		- Impacts the database
